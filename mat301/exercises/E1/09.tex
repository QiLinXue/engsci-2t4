\begin{sol}
    First, note that $a^2 = e \implies a = a^{-1}$. Then:
    \begin{align}
        & ab = ba \\ 
        \iff & (ab)(ba)^{-1} = e \\ 
        \iff & aba^{-1}b^{-1} = e \\ 
        \iff & abab = e \\ 
        \iff & (ab)^2 = e 
    \end{align}
    Since $ab \in G$, we must have $(ab)^2 = e \implies ab=ba$.
\end{sol}
